FORGE

Strength & Endurance · 7-Week Block

Block Structure
7 weeks. Weeks 1–6 progressive overload. Week 7 is a mandatory deload at 50% volume — this is where adaptation locks in.
Anchor vs Flex
Anchor exercises are fixed — they drive the program's core stimulus. Flex exercises can be swapped for equipment or preference without breaking the system.
Progressive Overload
Weight auto-calculates each week using your entered base weight. You show up, execute the reps, log the work. The system drives the progression.
Aerobic Base
Zone 2 Sunday builds the aerobic engine (133–145 bpm). Thursday tempo builds lactate threshold (146–158 bpm). Both are non-negotiable.
Leg Balance
Tuesday is quad-dominant (Squat leads). Saturday is glute and hamstring dominant (Hip Thrust leads). Different stimulus, same legs.
Deload Week
Week 7 is not optional. The body adapts during recovery, not during stress. Skip deload and you plateau. Run it and you come back stronger.
