Strength & Endurance · 7-Week Block

FORGE is a 7-day-a-week strength and endurance program built around a single principle: consistent, measurable progress across both lifting and running — in the same block, at the same time. Every day has a purpose. Nothing is filler.

Block Structure
6 weeks of progressive loading followed by a Week 7 deload. Weights auto-calculate each week — you show up and lift.
Anchor vs Flex
Anchor exercises are fixed and always lead. Flex exercises are draggable — reorder them to match equipment or energy on any given day.
Progressive Overload
Key lifts add load every week via a built-in delta. The app tracks it automatically — no spreadsheet needed.
Aerobic Base
Zone 2 on Sunday builds mitochondrial density over time. Thursday tempo develops lactate threshold on top of that base.
Leg Balance
Tuesday is quad dominant (Back Squat leads). Saturday is glute and hip dominant (Hip Thrust leads). Both days protect joint health as run mileage increases.
Deload Week
Week 7 holds at Week 6 weights with no new loading stress. The block is designed so the deload is earned, not optional.
